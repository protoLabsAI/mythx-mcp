# NPC Expansion

Expand a basic NPC concept into a detailed, GM-ready profile that is memorable and specific.

## Input Required

Provide the following context:
- **NPC Name**: The character's name
- **NPC Description**: Basic concept or role
- **World Context**: Setting, tone, relevant world details
- **Story Context**: Current plot situation
- **PC Context**: Who the player characters are and their relationship to this NPC

## Output Format

Create these sections:

### 1. Role (1 sentence)
- Function in the story (ally, antagonist, neutral, quest giver, complication)
- Why the PCs will interact with them

### 2. Personality (2-3 sentences)
- Key personality traits
- Distinctive speech patterns or mannerisms
- Memorable quirks or habits
- How they react under pressure

### 3. Motivation (1-2 sentences)
- What drives this character
- What they want from the PCs or the situation
- What they fear or avoid

### 4. Relationships (1-2 sentences)
- Connections to other NPCs
- Attitude toward the PCs
- Factional or family ties

### 5. Combat Profile
- Threat level: Non-combatant / Minor threat / Dangerous / Elite / Boss
- Fighting style if applicable
- Key abilities or weapons

### 6. Secret (1-2 sentences)
- Hidden agenda, past, weakness, or connection
- Something PCs could discover through investigation
- Creates complications or plot hooks

## Quality Standards

**Be specific and vivid, not generic.**

Bad example:
> "An evil demon. Very powerful. Wants to destroy things."

Good example:
> "The Merchant - A mid-level demon in a tailored suit who collects debts. Speaks in contract law terminology. Never raises voice, always polite, terrifyingly patient. Motivation: Maintain order in the underworld economy. Secret: Actually admires Lisa's integrity and might offer a fair trade if impressed."

Use concrete details, specific behaviors, and distinctive characteristics. The GM should be able to roleplay this character immediately.
